Jalapeño Poppers

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz Package of Cream Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Powdered Ranch Packet

Steps:

  1. Take a softened package of ranch powder and mix thoroughly with cream cheese
  2. Cut the tops off the jalapenos and cut them in half length wise, then scoop out all the seeds and "meat" of the jalapeno
  3. Fill each jalapeno with the mixed cream cheese
  4. Cut bacon to match the length of the jalapenos and place one slice over the top of each one
  5. Bake at 400F for 25-35 min or until the bacon is fully cooked
