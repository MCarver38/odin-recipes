Whipped Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • Splash of Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Take a metal bowl and the whisk you're going to use and place them into a freezer for 15 minutes
  2. Combine everything and whisk with a stand mixer or electric beater. Start slow and progessively mix faster as the mixture thickens
  3. Mix until soft peaks are achieved. Then manually whisk to the desired consistency, as to not accidentally overmix
  4. Refrigerate until used
