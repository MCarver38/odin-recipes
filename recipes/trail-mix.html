Trail Mix

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups Corn Chex
  • 5 cups Rice Chex
  • 1 Tbsp Seasoned Salt
  • 1 cup Peanuts
  • 3 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 cup Peanut M&Ms
  • 10 Tbsp Butter
  • Skinny Pretzels
  • Garlic Rye Chips
  • Garlic Powder

Steps:

  1. Combine cereal, peanuts, pretzels, and the rye chips in a large baking trey/pan
  2. Melt butter and then pour in the Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, and seasoned salt. Stir to combine
  3. Pour over the cereal mixture and stir to evenly coat
  4. Cook at 250F for 1.5 hours, stirring every 30 minutes
  5. Once it's done cooking, pour in M&Ms and stir to incorporate
